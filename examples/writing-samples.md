# Writing Samples

Use this file only for anonymized or public samples.

Recommended sample set:

- 1-2 polished long-form samples
- 1-2 emails or short notes
- 1-2 technical or professional passages
- 1 informal sample if the target letter should retain warmth

For each sample, include:

```text
SAMPLE_TITLE:
CONTEXT:
AUDIENCE:
DATE_OR_PERIOD:
TEXT:
```

Do not commit private writing samples, CVs, job descriptions, or personal application materials unless they are intentionally public.
